Sample input/output from the Aggregation Module


Turkers will be shown the summarized story so far, and are tasked with adding on to the story and then offering two potential
options for where the story can go.

Example:

Please read the story so far and add to it to bring it to a point where there is a crossroads between two options. Then 
provide those two options. 



Story so far:

You are a blacksmith working for the royal forge. The commoners aren't happy with the monarchy and you've
secretly been making weapons for a potential uprising. 
 

What the turker adds:

You decide to distribute the first batch you made, however as you're doing so a messenger approaches you and
tells you the king requests your presence.


Two options:

1) Follow him
2) Ignore him
